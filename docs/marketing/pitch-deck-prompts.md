# TrendyWheels — Pitch Deck Prompt-Pack

> A 12-slide investor / partner pitch deck for **TrendyWheels**, the Egypt-based
> marketplace and super-app for golf carts and light electric vehicles.
> Each slide ships with paste-ready copy and a custom, slide-specific
> image-generation prompt. The visual system is locked across all 12 frames so
> the deck reads as one cohesive set.

---

## How to use this pack

1. Each slide below has three parts: **(A)** title + one-line purpose, **(B)** headline + body copy you can paste straight into the slide, and **(C)** a copy-pasteable image prompt for that specific frame.
2. Generate **one 16:9 image per slide** (12 images total). Do not collapse multiple slides into one frame.
3. Keep the **Global Visual System** block (below) prepended or appended to every image prompt so the palette, type, grid, and treatment stay identical across the deck.
4. Bracketed placeholders like `[X regions]` are deliberate — replace them with real numbers before presenting. Do not invent figures.

## GLOBAL VISUAL SYSTEM (prepend to every image prompt)

**Brand:** TrendyWheels — premium, editorial, eco-forward mobility brand for golf carts and light EVs in Egypt (Cairo gated compounds + North Coast / Red Sea coastal resorts).

**Palette — use these EXACT values, no substitutions:**

- `trustWorth` deep navy `#02011F` — primary dark canvas / ink
- `friendlyBlue` `#2B0FF8` — primary brand accent (electric, confident)
- `trendyPink` `#FF0065` — energetic highlight / CTA accent
- `ecoLimelight` `#A9F453` — eco / "go" / charge accent
- `poolBlue` `#00C7EA` — secondary cool accent / water + sky
- `white` `#FFFFFF` — light surfaces, type on dark

**Palette discipline:** One palette across all 12 slides. On any single slide use the deep-navy canvas (or clean white) plus a maximum of TWO accent colors so frames feel intentional, not rainbow. `friendlyBlue` is the recurring anchor; `trendyPink` is reserved for emphasis and CTAs; `ecoLimelight` signals charge/eco/positive; `poolBlue` reads as sky/water/coastal. No purple-blue AI-glow gradients, no neon halos — only low-chroma, palette-matched tonal grades and clean color blocks.

**Typography system:** Modern grotesk display (Neue-Montreal / Satoshi feel) for headlines — tight tracking, confident scale contrast, mostly medium weight, never six-line wraps. A clean geometric sans for body and labels. One type family world across all slides. Headlines large and sparse; body copy short and readable. Optional small Arabic accent type (Cairo / Tajawal feel) paired tastefully where an Egyptian touch helps — never machine-translated gibberish, keep Arabic minimal or omit if uncertain.

**Grid + layout system:** 12-column editorial grid, generous gutters, deep margins, lots of negative space. Consistent border-radius language on cards and UI (soft 16–20px rounded rectangles). Thin hairline rules in a low-opacity accent. Tiny footer detail on every slide: small wordmark lockup bottom-left, slide number bottom-right (e.g. `01 / 12`), set in the body sans at small size. A subtle micro-grain / fine-noise texture over flat fields for premium tactile depth. Text always sits in a clean, high-contrast safe area — never over busy image detail.

**Visual treatment:** Editorial photography and product imagery, art-directed, color-graded toward the brand palette. Golf carts and light EVs rendered as desirable design objects — clean studio crops or cinematic compound/resort context (palm-lined compound streets, North Coast white architecture, dusk pool-blue skies). Motion-implied energy: staggered float-up and cinematic fade-through. Cohesive duotone / palette-locked grading on photographic frames. Aspect ratio **16:9** for every slide.

**Anti-slop guardrails:** No generic stock office people, no cliché robot/AI imagery, no floating blobs or orbs, no glassmorphism pile-ups, no fake dense dashboards, no gradient-text shortcut. Premium restraint over decoration.
